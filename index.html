Rocket Rescue

About

Rocket Rescue is a 2D platformer where the player must avoid environmental obsticales while traversing a foreign planet to get to their ship. The planet is much more hostile than Earth, with acid rain, acid puddles, and falling rubble. The player must navigate these obsticales and learn how to survive to make it to their rocket.

How I Made It

To make Rocket Rescue, I used Unity. I made all the art myself using Procreate. This project was made in 72 hours for a Hackathon I participated in. To see the code, click the link to my Github repository.
